Shown in TimeScope → Settings → Extension connection (default 48733).
Copy from TimeScope → Settings → Extension connection.
These domains never leave your browser — not even to the local app.
What this extension sends: only the active tab's domain (e.g. youtube.com) with start/end timestamps, to your own computer at 127.0.0.1. It never reads or sends page text, full URLs, titles, forms, passwords, or history — and nothing is sent to the internet.
